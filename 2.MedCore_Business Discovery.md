------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Problem Statement (Layman Terms)

Today, clinics manage patient records in different ways:

-   Some use Excel sheets\
-   Some use paper files\
-   Some use small local software\
-   Data is scattered and not centralized

Because of this:

-   Patient information is hard to find quickly\
-   Records can be lost or inconsistent\
-   Security of sensitive patient data is not guaranteed\
-   Clinics cannot easily scale operations\
-   Reporting and auditing are difficult

In simple terms:

> Clinics need one secure, easy-to-use online system where staff can
> safely store and access patient information from anywhere.

The goal is to build a secure web-based system that solves this problem.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Business Vision

Create a centralized, secure, and scalable platform that:

-   Standardizes patient record management\
-   Improves operational efficiency\
-   Reduces manual errors\
-   Protects sensitive patient data\
-   Supports growth to more clinics in future

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Target Users

-   Doctors\
-   Receptionists\
-   Clinic Administrators

Estimated initial users: 250\
Peak concurrent users: \~60

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. Business Goals

-   Centralized patient data management\
-   Secure authentication for staff\
-   Role-based access control\
-   Ability to expand to 100+ clinics in future

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. Initial Constraints

-   MVP launch within 3 months\
-   Must run on Azure cloud\
-   Moderate budget (cost-conscious solution required)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 7. High-Level Success Criteria

-   System successfully deployed in 20 clinics\
-   Secure login enforced (MFA enabled)\
-   No major data security incidents\
-   Stable performance under expected load\
-   Positive feedback from clinic staff
